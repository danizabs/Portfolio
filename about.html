Danielle Zabielski is currently studying Communications, Advertising/Promotions, and Art at Saint Joseph's University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Her interest in the ways in which people communicate has lead to her involvement in radio, blogging, journalism, freelance writing, photography, and advertising.

Danielle is Assistant Programming Manager of the student-run radio station, Radio 1851, and a Staff Writer for The Hawk, SJU's student-run newspaper. She is a student blogger for SJU, a new contributor to Femsplain, and runs this personal blog in her spare time.
